About tekula feeding project

Tekula Feeding Project is a vital feeding founded by Hilde Penepandula Fillemon, a young Namibian women and Co founder Matheus Elago Shigwedha. This initiative focuses on involving communities and schools from marginalized and struggling areas to combat malnutrition and starvation. 

In response to the rising number of  deaths due to malnutrition in Namibia, Tekula is dedicated to ensuring that every Namibian receive  proper nutrition and that no child dies from malnutrition.
